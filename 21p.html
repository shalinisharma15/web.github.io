Nir Nikunj Water Park

The water park is located closer to Tara Mandal in Rail Vihar Colony. A visit of one day at this amusement water park in Gorakhpur is worth it.

What travellers are saying

It is good place for enjoying high class picinic .Here we have enjoy boating and natiral scenes.

Neer Nikunj Water Park Ticket Price/Entry fee

From 10 am to 8 pm Monday to Saturday : Rs.650/- per person Sunday, Public Holidays & Festivals : Rs.750/- per person From 5 pm to 8 pm Monday to Saturday : Rs.320/- per person Sunday, Public Holidays & Festivals : Rs.425/- per person

Do water parks in Gorakhpur have a fixed dress code for visitors?

Yes, water parks require you to be suitably dressed in swimwear or other specific fabrics while entering the pool
